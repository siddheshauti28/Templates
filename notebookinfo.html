City Watch: Moscow

With its artistry,history and majesty, Moscow, the Russian capital, never ceases to inspire and delight its visitor. An ideal city that tantalizes your senses and soothes your spirit, Moscow balances the ancient and modern with elan. Major sights like the Kremlin and St. Basil's Cathedral are on most visitors itineraries and no matter what your interests you'll probabaly find something to marvel about here.
